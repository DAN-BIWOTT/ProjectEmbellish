Who we are

Embellish is a group of young lawyers -and other professionals- who are prepared to take the bull by its horns and champion progressive thinking, exchange of ideas and networking in all forms. What makes us different is that we do not just stop at ideas, but aim at taking action

Our core goal is to effect social change using both conventional and unconventional routes availed to us by our training and interests. Our activities include quarterly Roundtable meetups, an annual gala dinner, as well as various short-term community impact projects throughout the year. Part of our output is a quarterly publication on emerging areas of law and other pertinent issues as well as a Podcast capturing current affairs, governance, and other areas of interest.

WHO WE ARE LOOKING FOR

We are open to all professionals with a keen interest in impacting the society beyond their day-to-day role. We particularly welcome young professionals keen on meeting other like-minded individuals and sharing ideas.

How to Join

Embellish exists entirely online. We have a WhatsApp channel, as well as social media handles including X, Instagram, LinkedIn and TikTok. You may join our mailing list for updates on what members of the community are up to, our activities, opportunities and more.

NEWSLETTER

This is a quarterly publication whose main goal is to keep legal professionals abreast of developments in the sector, to encourage expression of ideas and to provide a platform for those who express themselves best through writing.
The scope of the Newsletter is legal, political and lifestyle, with the aim being to demonstrate a work-life integration and to celebrate every aspect of life and the unique culture in which we operate.
You can reach the Newsletter Team via embellishnewsletter@gmail.com

THE ROUNDTABLE

We are a group of professionals that stepped into the exciting world of practice right from virtual classes.
We have maintained a vibrant existence and have done our best to connect virtually. The Roundtable is an opportunity for young professionals to network and meet physically, which contributes to exchange of ideas and general wellbeing.
A silent lawyer is a dangerous thing, we like to say. Conversations with like-minded people leads to creation of new ideas. The goal of the Roundtable is to educate, to expose and to challenge young professionals to think beyond the ordinary. The sessions are structured and themed, with experts and professionals in specific areas giving tips and tricks to attendees.
