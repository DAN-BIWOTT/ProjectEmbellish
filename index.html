NEWSLETTER

This is a quarterly publication whose main goal is to keep legal professionals abreast of developments in the sector, to encourage expression of ideas and to provide a platform for those who express themselves best through writing. The scope of the Newsletter is legal, political and lifestyle, with the aim being to demonstrate a work-life integration and to celebrate every aspect of life and the unique culture in which we operate. You can reach the Newsletter Team via embellishnewsletter@gmail.com

THE ROUNDTABLE

We are a group of professionals that stepped into the exciting world of practice right from virtual classes. We have maintained a vibrant existence and have done our best to connect virtually. The Roundtable is an opportunity for young professionals to network and meet physically, which contributes to exchange of ideas and general wellbeing. A silent lawyer is a dangerous thing, we like to say. Conversations with like-minded people leads to creation of new ideas. The goal of the Roundtable is to educate, to expose and to challenge young professionals to think beyond the ordinary. The sessions are structured and themed, with experts and professionals in specific areas giving tips and tricks to attendees.
